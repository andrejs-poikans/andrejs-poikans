    • default den haag is a non-profit, artist-run, curatorial initiative that aims to present and nurture significant creative contemporary sound and performance in the Hague, the Netherlands, which due to their experimental, emerging or persistent other-minded nature are located outside the dominant and commercial music culture/industry. Since the end of 2022, default has been setting up their nomadic sound series in the Hague, with regular events combining a handful of experimental music and sound art performances each time, next to occasionally hosting accompanying talks and lectures.

      Our programs hold a specific international focus on developments affiliated with electro-acoustic improvisation, experimental contemporary composition and performance, sound art performance, computer music, and all sorts of sound poetics. This includes creative work in relation to sound, by artists who work across disciplines.

      default has curated and set up events at and in collaboration with: The Grey Space in the Middle, 3345 The Hague, Central Library, The Institute of Sonology, and iii.

    • General inquiries can be directed to info@defaultdenhaag.com

      If you have any questions about accessibility or require any support when visiting one of our events, please contact us in advance of your visit.

      We try to keep ticket prices affordable, but in case your current financial situation does not allow you to visit the event, but you are keen on coming, you can mail to info@defaultdenhaag.com and we can find a solution.

      If you want to get involved as a volunteer at default, contact us at vol@defaultdenhaag.com — we are always in need of people to help with selling tickets or hanging posters.

      If you are aware of our programming and think your proposal is suitable for our curation, feel free to send a short description with link to audio or video to: program@defaultdenhaag.com Please be aware that because of how our operation functions at the moment, it’s not always possible to get back to all programming enquiries in time - thank you for understanding. We are not a venue but a strongly curated series with a limited amount of events per year, relying still on a lot of voluntary work and with a limited amount of time and means.
